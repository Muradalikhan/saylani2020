How to make pizza?

Pizza Ingredients

  • Fresh Dough. Unbleached enriched wheat flour, water, sugar, soybean oil, salt, yeast
  • Cheese. Part-skim mozzarella cheese (pasteurized milk, cultures, salt, enzymes), modified food starch, sugarcane fiber, whey protein concentrate, sodium citrate
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Thin Crust
  • Gluten-Free Dough
  1. Fresh Dough. Unbleached enriched wheat flour, water, sugar, soybean oil, salt, yeast (should be taken)
  2. Cheese. Part-skim mozzarella cheese (pasteurized milk, cultures, salt, enzymes), modified food starch, sugarcane fiber, whey protein concentrate, sodium citrate (should be mixed)
  3. Pizza Sauce (should be put then )
  4. Thin Crust (should be put then )
  5. Gluten-Free Dough (should be put )

Pizza will be ready within 5 minutes
